Privacy Policy

Optidose Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026

Who we are

Optidose ("we", "us") is a supplement tracking app developed by Optidose™ / Alex Chopoyhlu, contactable at alex.chopoyhlu@outlook.com.

What this app is

Optidose helps you track supplements and provides general, educational information about commonly cited dosages. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. See the in-app Medical Disclaimer.

Data we process

Stored on your device and in your private iCloud account (via Apple CloudKit)

  • Your profile inputs (goals, gender, age range, weight range, training frequency, sun exposure, diet, supplement experience).
  • The supplements you track, dosages, schedules, reminders, logged doses, and any side effects you record.

This data is synced through your own iCloud account using Apple CloudKit. We do not operate our own servers that store this data, and we cannot read your iCloud data.

Health data (HealthKit)

  • With your explicit permission, the app reads health metrics (e.g. step count) to personalise supplement suggestions.
  • HealthKit data is processed on your device only. It is never transmitted off the device, never sold, and never used for advertising.
  • You can revoke this access at any time in the iOS Health app or Settings.

Subscriptions

  • Purchases are processed by Apple and (if enabled) managed via RevenueCat. We receive subscription status (active / inactive), not your payment details. See Apple's and RevenueCat's privacy policies.

Notifications

  • If you enable reminders, scheduling happens on your device. We do not operate a push server that receives your supplement data.

How we use data

  • To run the app's tracking, reminders, and educational dosage features.
  • To personalise suggestions to your profile and (with permission) health data.

We do not sell your personal data or use it for third-party advertising.

Third parties

  • Apple (iCloud/CloudKit, HealthKit, App Store / StoreKit) — see Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat (subscription management), if enabled — see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Data retention & deletion

Your tracking data lives on your device and in your iCloud account. Deleting the app and removing its iCloud data removes it. To request any assistance, contact alex.chopoyhlu@outlook.com.

Your rights

Depending on your location (e.g. UK/EU GDPR, California CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact alex.chopoyhlu@outlook.com to exercise them.

Children

Optidose is not directed at children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy and will revise the "Last updated" date above.
